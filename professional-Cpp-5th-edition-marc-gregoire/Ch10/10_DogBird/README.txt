Two standalone programs, each with its own main():
  - 01_DogBird.cpp - multiple inheritance with ambiguous eat().
  - 02_Diamond.cpp - the diamond shape with a common Animal base.

Compile each file separately, e.g.:
  cl /std:c++latest /EHsc 01_DogBird.cpp
  cl /std:c++latest /EHsc 02_Diamond.cpp
